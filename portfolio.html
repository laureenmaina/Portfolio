About Me

Laurine Maina

Hello! I'm Laurine Maina, a passionate Software Engineer with a strong foundation in backend development and a growing interest in full-stack technologies. I am currently pursuing a Software Engineering program at Power Learn Project. I was previously in Moringa School where I’ve honed my skills in Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and React. My journey in software engineering began with a desire to solve real-world problems and create impactful, user-focused solutions. Over time, I’ve developed a keen interest in building scalable applications, seamless integrations (such as M-Pesa payment systems), and working with both relational and non-relational databases. What drives me is the constant evolution of technology and the endless opportunities to learn and innovate. Whether it's building APIs, crafting efficient data models, or enhancing user experiences, I thrive in dynamic environments that challenge me to grow both professionally and personally. Outside of tech, I enjoy exploring thrilling crime and thriller movies, adventurous activities, and contributing to open-source projects. I’m always open to collaborating on meaningful projects or exploring new technologies that push boundaries. Let’s connect and create something impactful!
